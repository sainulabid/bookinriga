About RigaNest

RigaNest is a small, independently run collection of apartments scattered across Riga — from quiet Old Town courtyards to leafy streets near Vērmanes Garden — each one chosen and furnished with an actual stay in mind, not just a photo.

Whether you're passing through for a weekend, working remotely for a month, or bringing the whole family for a proper holiday, we try to keep things simple: clear photos, honest descriptions, and a booking flow that doesn't make you jump through hoops.

Every apartment is cleaned and checked between guests, and comes stocked with the basics you'd want on arrival — fast WiFi, fresh linens, and a kitchen you can actually cook in.

Our apartments sit in Riga's Old Town and the surrounding city centre, all within easy walking distance of the main sights, museums and the best places to eat.

Studios for solo trips, two-bedroom flats for families — there's something for most kinds of stay.

And if anything comes up before, during or after your stay, our small team is reachable and genuinely happy to help.
