BookinRiga ir neliela, neatkarīgi pārvaldīta apartamentu kolekcija, kas izkaisīta pa visu Rīgu — no klusiem Vecrīgas pagalmiem līdz zaļām ielām pie Vērmanes dārza — katrs izvēlēts un iekārtots ar domu par reālu uzturēšanos, nevis tikai skaistu fotogrāfiju.

BookinRiga is a small, independently run collection of apartments scattered across Riga — from quiet Old Town courtyards to leafy streets near Vērmanes Garden — each one chosen and furnished with an actual stay in mind, not just a photo.

Whether you're passing through for a weekend, working remotely for a month, or bringing the whole family for a proper holiday, we try to keep things simple: clear photos, honest descriptions, and a booking flow that doesn't make you jump through hoops.

Every apartment is cleaned and checked between guests, and comes stocked with the basics you'd want on arrival — fast WiFi, fresh linens, and a kitchen you can actually cook in.
